Prototype Walkthrough

Below are the key screens of the ChooZ prototype with descriptions.

🏠 Home

Greeting, Today’s Overview, Recent Decisions (color‑coded), Random Pick, FAB (+).

➕ Add Decision

Form to log a new decision: title, options, chosen option, category, notes, date/time.

📋 History

Searchable, filterable list of past decisions. Tap to view details.

🔍 Decision Detail

Full view: title, all options, chosen option (highlighted), timestamp, notes, edit button.

🏷️ Categories

Manage categories: add, edit, delete. Colors are consistent across the app.

📊 Statistics

Charts for decisions per category and time‑of‑day patterns.

⚙️ Settings

Dark mode toggle, notifications, account info, random pick link.

🎲 Random Pick

Overlay that suggests a random choice. “Log this” pre‑fills Add Decision screen.
