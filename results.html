Usability Testing Results

📊 System Usability Scale (SUS) Scores

We collected 10 valid responses. The average SUS score is 78.5 / 100 (above the industry average of 68).

  • Highest score: 87.5
  • Lowest score: 62.5
  • Standard deviation: 8.2
💬 Key Issues from Open‑ended Feedback

What participants liked:

  • “The color‑coded categories made scanning very easy.”
  • “Logging a decision is quick and straightforward.”
  • “Random pick is a fun feature – helped me decide when I was stuck.”

What participants struggled with:

  • “No confirmation when I save a decision – I wasn’t sure it worked.”
  • “The random pick overlay didn’t have a visible close button.”
  • “I didn’t understand the category colors at first – needed a legend.”
