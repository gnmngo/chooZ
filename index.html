Usability Evaluation Report | HCI2 – Group 1

About the Project

ChooZ is a mobile app that helps users log, organize, and reflect on daily micro‑decisions – from what to eat to what to wear. This website documents the heuristic evaluation (Nielsen’s 10 heuristics) and the usability testing (SUS questionnaire + open‑ended feedback) conducted with 10 participants.
